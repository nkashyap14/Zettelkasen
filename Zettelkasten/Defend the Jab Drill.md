# Defend the Jab Drill


- From perspective of versus orthodox
- Inside is towards jab hand
- Outside is towards cross

#### Defensive Options Southpaw:
	- Block
	- Slip to inside
	- Parry with lead hand
	- Pull back


#### Defensive Options Orthodox:
	- Block
	- Slip to outside
	- Parry with rear hand
	- Pull back
