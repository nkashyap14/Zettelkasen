# Continuous Uniform Random Variable

- Says all x's are equally likely over a range
$$
f_X(x) = a<= x <=b
$$
$$
E[X] = \frac{a + b}{2}
$$
$$
var(X) = \int_{a}^{b}(x - \frac{a + b}{2})^2 * \frac{1}{b -a}*dx = \frac{(b-a)^2}{12}
$$
