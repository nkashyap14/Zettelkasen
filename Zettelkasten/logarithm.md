# logarithm

- A logarithm answers the question to what exponent must a given base be raised to produce a certain number
$$
log_b(x) = y
$$
	- b is the base
	- x is the number
	- y is the exponent
	- Above is equivalent to statement below
$$
b^y = x
$$
